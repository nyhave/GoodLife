Solution: Multi-Factor Confirmation System

  • Volume Confirmation: Breakout candle volume must be ≥1.5x opening range average
  • VWAP Confirmation: Price must be aligned with VWAP direction
    • LONG: Price above VWAP
    • SHORT: Price below VWAP
  • Sentiment Confirmation: Pre-market sentiment must align with trade direction
    • LONG: Positive sentiment (score > 0)
    • SHORT: Negative sentiment (score < 0)
  • Minimum Threshold: By default, 2 of 3 confirmations required (configurable)
